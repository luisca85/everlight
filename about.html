Our Story

Born from a love
of real moments.

Everlight was founded on the belief that the most meaningful photographs are the ones that feel true — to the day, to the couple, to the quiet beauty of what love actually looks like.

Our Founder

Andrea
Romero

Melbourne-based photographer and studio owner specialising in family, maternity and newborn photography. After years documenting life's most meaningful moments, Andrea expanded her creative vision into weddings — bringing the same natural, timeless and authentic approach to couples on their most important day.

Her philosophy is simple: photography should feel like a conversation, not a production. She guides couples with warmth, patience and an eye trained to find beauty in the unscripted.
