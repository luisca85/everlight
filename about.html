Our Founder

Andrea
Romero

I’m a Colombian photographer with over 7 years of experience, now based in Australia, where I run a photography studio focused on families, children, maternity, and newborn sessions.

Over the years, my passion for capturing real, meaningful moments has grown beyond the studio. That’s what led me to expand into weddings and events—spaces where emotions unfold naturally and every story is unique. Everlight was created with a clear purpose: to bring together talented photographers who have strong professional skills but may not always have the time or platform to connect with clients. By building this network, we combine experience, creativity, and opportunity into one shared vision.
