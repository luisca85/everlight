03 Common Questions

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 9–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (October–March). That said, reach out regardless — we occasionally have last-minute availability.

Do you travel for weddings?

Yes — we cover Sydney, Melbourne, Byron Bay and surrounding regions. Travel fees may apply for locations outside metropolitan areas. We also travel internationally for destination weddings.

When will we receive our images?

You'll receive a preview gallery within 7 days of your wedding. Your full edited gallery is delivered within 6–8 weeks, via a private online gallery with download access.

Can we request custom packages?

Every wedding is different. If none of our collections feel like the right fit, get in touch — we're happy to tailor a package to suit your specific day and needs.
